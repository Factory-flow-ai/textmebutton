The fix is absurdly simple

No app. No battery. No thinking. Just press.

1

Stick the button next to your pills

Peel the adhesive backing. Stick it on the medicine cabinet, the nightstand, or right on the pill organizer. Takes 2 seconds.

2

Take your dose, press the button

One firm press. The button powers itself from the press — no batteries, ever. It sends a signal to the hub connected to your router.

3

Get a timestamped text

Your phone buzzes: "Medicine taken at 7:42am." Can't remember later? Just scroll your texts. The proof is right there.

Why this works when apps don't

The best system is the one you actually use.

🎯

Zero friction

No unlocking a phone, no opening an app, no typing. Just press. The physical action connects to the physical habit of taking your pills.

💬

SMS is permanent

App notifications disappear when swiped. Texts stay in your message thread forever. Scroll back anytime to see exactly when you took each dose.

♾️

Always ready

No batteries to change. No charger needed. No software updates. The button works the same on day 1 as it will on year 20. Set it and truly forget it.

Who this is for

Executive function makes routine tracking hard. TextMeButton removes the cognitive load entirely — there's nothing to remember except "press after you swallow."

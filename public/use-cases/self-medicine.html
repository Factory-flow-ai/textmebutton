🔬 How it actually works

No battery. No charging. No solar. Here's why.

⚡ Kinetic energy harvesting

Your finger press drives a magnet past a copper coil inside the button, generating electricity on the spot. Same principle as dynamo flashlights — miniaturized into a 2.5-inch disc.

📡 Wireless signal to hub

The generated energy powers a 433 MHz radio burst that reaches the Ethernet hub up to 150ft+ away — through walls, floors, and doors.

📱 Hub sends the text

The hub connects to your router via Ethernet and sends an SMS to your configured phone numbers. Real text message, any carrier, any phone.

♾️ 20+ years, zero maintenance

No battery to die. No charging to forget. No solar panel to clean. Install once, it works for decades. Proven technology used in commercial buildings worldwide.

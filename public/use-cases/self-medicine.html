How it actually works

The engineering behind zero-maintenance simplicity.

⚡

Kinetic energy harvesting

A magnet passes a copper coil when you press — the dynamo principle in a 2.5-inch disc. Your finger powers every transmission.

📡

Wireless signal to hub

433 MHz radio signal reaches 150+ feet through walls, floors, and ceilings. One press, instant relay to your hub.

💬

Hub sends the text

The hub connects via Ethernet to your router and sends a real SMS to any carrier. No cloud subscription, no monthly fee.

♾

20+ years zero maintenance

No battery to replace. No charging cable. No solar panel. The button self-powers from the energy of your press, indefinitely.
